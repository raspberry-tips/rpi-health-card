# Raspberry Pi Health Card

Welcome to the ultimate, clean dashboard card for your Raspberry Pi Home Assistant server.

This card gives you a quick, beautiful overview of:
- 🌡️ CPU Temperature
- 🧠 RAM Usage
- 💽 Disk Space
- ⚡ Power Status (Under-Voltage Warnings)

If your Pi suffers from power supply issues, this card will display a red warning box and guide you to a [Power Calculator Tool](https://raspberry.tips/?page_id=7948) to find the correct 15W or 27W power supply.

### Why this card?
Many users don't notice when their Raspberry Pi throttles its CPU due to a weak power supply. This card brings that hidden entity right to the surface of your dashboard.

For more deep-dives into Home Assistant on the Raspberry Pi, visit the official [Pillar Page on raspberry.tips](https://raspberry.tips/home-assistant-auf-dem-raspberry-pi-der-grosse-smart-home-hub-2026).
